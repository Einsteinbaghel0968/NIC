About NIC Institute

NIC (National Institute of Computer) is an Institute of Professional Studies, ISO 9001:2015 Certified, committed to developing individuals into complete professionals. Since 1994, we have been in the service of humanity, providing high-quality education to empower students with technical and life skills.

Our training method, Smart Labs Plus, ensures you learn better by participating and doing, rather than just listening to lectures. We also teach Marketing, Sales, and HRD to enhance your capabilities, preparing you to face life’s challenges confidently and emerge stronger and successful.

A high-quality education is one of the most important things we can provide. Exposure to diverse topics, people, and experiences contributes to a fulfilling institute experience. NIC is managed by the Cyber Information Technology Society, formed under Society Act XXI of 1860, with VO/NGO registration DL/2009/0023143 (NITI Aayog, Govt. of India).
