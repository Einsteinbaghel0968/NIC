Details of Junior Basic Training (JBT) Course

Junior Basic Training (JBT) is a diploma level course with a duration of 2 years. Child Development, Educational Technology, Teaching of Mathematics, etc are some of the areas covered under this course. Admission to the course is offered either on the basis of qualifying HSC exam or by entrance test. JBT CET is the entrance test held by Himachal Pradesh Board of Education for admission to this course. After pursuing the course, one can apply for the position of teacher in primary or middle schools. They can also work in the role of Student Counsellors, Educational Administrators, etc.

Subjects Covered Under Junior Basic Training (JBT) Course

  • Child Development
  • Educational Technology
  • Health & Physical Education
  • Philosophical Perspective of Education
  • Sociological Perspective Technology
  • Teaching of Art Education
  • Teaching of English
  • Teaching of Environmental Studies (Science)
  • Teaching of EVS Social Science

  • Teaching of Mathematics
